Game Engines

Most of the games I've made so far have been built using Unity, but I'm actively learning Unreal Engine. I hope to update this in the future when im 100% confident in both!

Systems & Architecture

Nothing compares to the joy of watching systems respond to each other and click together like a never-ending puzzle. I prefer having code that doesn't fight me as it grows because it will most likely win. Modularity and efficiency is the golden key.
